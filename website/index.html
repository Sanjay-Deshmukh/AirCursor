Features

Everything You Need,
Nothing You Don't

AirCursor packs powerful gesture recognition into a lightweight, easy-to-use application.

Cursor Control

Move your mouse cursor freely by simply moving your hand in front of the webcam. Smooth, responsive, and precise.

Click Gestures

Perform left clicks with a simple pinch gesture. Natural and intuitive — no learning curve required.

Scroll Support

Scroll up and down effortlessly using finger gestures. Browse pages, documents, and feeds hands-free.

Screenshots

Take screenshots instantly with a two-fist gesture. Images are saved automatically with timestamps.

Window Management

Switch between tabs, close applications, and manage windows with intuitive two-hand gestures.

Webcam Only

No expensive hardware needed. AirCursor works with any standard webcam that comes with your laptop or PC.

See It In Action

AI-Powered
Gesture Engine

AirCursor uses MediaPipe's state-of-the-art hand tracking technology to detect 21 hand landmarks in real-time, mapping your finger movements to precise on-screen actions.

  • Real-time 21-point hand landmark detection
  • Smooth cursor with exponential smoothing
  • Multi-hand gesture support
  • Low-latency processing at 60fps
